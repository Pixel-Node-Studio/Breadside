Our Story

At Bread Side, we blend traditional baking techniques with modern passion. Every morning, our ovens fill with the aroma of freshly baked bread, croissants, and pastries made from locally sourced ingredients.

Located in the vibrant neighborhood of Galatsi, we're your cozy corner for exceptional coffee, warm pastries, and genuine hospitality.
